Building Simple Shelves



What you will need

  • Glue
  • Drill
  • L brackets
  • hooks
  • Piece of wood
  • Decoration for the Top
  • Paint





Glue the board and decoration together

Use wood glue to put together your two pieces of wood



Next Add the L brackets

Using the drill screw on the two L Brackets. This is how we will put it on the wall later



Then Add the Hooks

Pre drill holes for the hooks and then screw them in by hand.



Time to Paint

This is the fun part. You can use paint, spray paint, glitter, and stickers! When you are finished use the L brackets to attack to the wall.
